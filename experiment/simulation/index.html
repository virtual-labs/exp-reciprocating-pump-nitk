DOUBLE ACTING RECIPROCATING PUMP

Objective

To conduct a performance test on double acting reciprocating pump.

Apparatus used:

Reciprocating Pump Setup with Motor, Collecting Tank, Spring Balance, Pressure Gauge, Stopwatch.


 Description Reciprocating Pump: is a positive displacement pump, it operates on the principle of actual displacement or pushing of liquid by a piston or plunger that executes a reciprocating motion in a closely fitted cylinder. The liquid is alternatively drawn from the pump and filled into suction side of the cyliner. The liquid fed to discharge side of the cylinder and emptied to the delivery pipe.

Delivery Head: is defined as the work that needs to be performed by the pump to pump the medium with reference to a defined unit of weight.

Suction Head: is the term used to describe liquid pressure at pump suction in terms of height of liquid column.
